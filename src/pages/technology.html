Technologies of the magic of Golovinski

The term "slab" refers to a flat cut of natural stone with specific patterns. Each piece is unique, like a fingerprint of nature's grandeur, born in its depths. Today, these blanks are highly popular for creating furniture elements, decorating spaces, and construction. Stone slabs attract not only luxury connoisseurs but also those who love natural materials with characteristic textures of natural minerals

They have a long lifespan and the ability to organically fit into any interior: modern, classic, loft, Scandinavian, Gothic, modern, country, etc. The key is to select the material that matches the color and texture of the room's design.

Features of creating Golivinsky slabs

  • Stone extraction 01 ]

    • The stone is extracted from the quarry using the latest cutting technologies, such as DAZZINI wire saw machines, Sandvik Tamrock and DAZZINI PCT 100 self-propelled drilling rigs. The extracted stone is transported to the production site using VOLVO F350 and VOLVO E220 front loaders. Automated block-turning devices from FRAVIZEL are used to preserve the integrity and quality of the raw material.

  • Block sawing 02 ]

    • The extracted stone in the form of large blocks is processed with fully automated equipment from the Italian manufacturer PEDRINI. The first stage is block squaring using the PEDRINI JUPITER GS210, which ensures the correct geometry of the future slab. The company cares for the environment by purifying the water used for stone cutting with advanced equipment from TECNOIDEA. The squared blocks are sawn into thinner slabs using PEDRINI MULTIWIRE JUPITER GS 230, which performs fast block cutting with 72 diamond wires, producing slabs from 2 to 10 centimeters thick.

  • Grinding and polishing 03 ]

    • The sawn slabs undergo a meticulous grinding and polishing process to give them a smooth and shiny surface, revealing the echo of the stone's soul, birthed by nature itself. At our company, the PEDRINI GALAXY B220GX line, equipped with 20 working heads, is used for polishing. This advanced equipment effectively removes all residual defects, giving the slabs incredible shine and sophistication. Thanks to this technology, each slab becomes a true work of art, embodying grandeur and beauty.

  • Cutting and shaping 04 ]

    • After polishing, the slabs are carefully trimmed to the required dimensions using high-precision equipment, including diamond saws and milling machines. This process demands exceptional accuracy and skill to ensure each slab meets the specified parameters and shapes. The use of advanced technologies allows achieving perfect geometry and flawless edges, ensuring the final product not only meets the highest quality standards but also exceeds client expectations. Each cutting stage is performed with maximum attention to detail, emphasizing the luxury and elegance of natural stone.

  • Quality check 05 ]

    • Each slab undergoes a thorough quality check to identify the slightest defects, cracks, or other imperfections. Our quality control process is extremely strict and detailed, ensuring that only the best products reach our clients' hands. All slabs that do not meet the high-quality standards are rejected or subjected to additional processing to eliminate the detected imperfections. Thus, we ensure that every slab we offer is a model of perfection and impeccable craftsmanship.

  • Surface treatment 06 ]

    • Depending on the client's requirements, the slab surface can be additionally treated, including the application of protective coatings, resins, and pigments, as well as reinforcing the back side of the slab with a special mesh for reliability and durability. For this, we use the advanced PEDRINI RESINLINE equipment, which significantly enhances the mechanical and aesthetic characteristics of the materials. Thanks to this technology, each slab becomes not only beautiful but also durable, ready for use in any conditions, highlighting the luxury and uniqueness of natural stone.

  • Packaging and transportation 07 ]

    • Finished slabs are carefully packed for reliable protection against mechanical damage during transportation. Loading is performed using high-tech equipment from leading manufacturers FRAVIZEL and MITSUBISHI MOTORS, guaranteeing the safety and preservation of each slab at all stages of delivery. This process ensures that our exquisite stone products arrive to clients in perfect condition, ready to become part of their elegant interiors.
