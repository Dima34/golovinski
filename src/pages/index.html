/ About Us

We Golovinski are the creators of your unparalleled experience, where the magic of elite natural materials intertwines with international-class service.

Founded in 1894, our company is the oldest stone processing enterprise in Ukraine, distinguished by the exceptional "Volga Blue" labradorite, which has become a symbol of our craftsmanship. We take pride in having consolidated over ten granite quarries, enabling us to serve clients worldwide.

Our company not only preserves a rich heritage, reflecting the values and principles of masterful service but also implements innovative and eco-friendly methods, allowing us to lead the industry and ensure the highest level of quality and reliability in our products.
